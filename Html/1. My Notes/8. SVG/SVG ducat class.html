Graphics software-coreldraw, photoshop, illustrator etc.
Graphics are of 2 types-
1)Raster/Bitmap- These graphics are made up of dots or pixels. That's why they are pixelate when zoom.
 All camera pics are raster by default. eg. Photoshop 
    
2)Vector- These are made up of curves or lines. 
That's why don't pixelate when zoom. eg. Coreldraw(used for logo design)
