Check if a triangle is equilateral, scalene, or isosceles

According to the lengths of their sides, triangles can be classified into three types which are:

          1.  Scalene
          2.  Isosceles
          3.  Equilateral

Scalene Triangle

A scalene triangle has all side lengths of different measures. No side will be equal in length to any of the other sides in such a triangle.

Isosceles Triangle

In an isosceles triangle, the lengths of two of the three sides are equal. So, the angles opposite the equal sides are equal to each other.

Equilateral Triangle

In an equilateral triangle, all the lengths of the sides are equal. In such a case, each of the interior angles will have a measure of 60 degrees. 



<script>

        if((side1 == side2) && (side1 == side3)){
