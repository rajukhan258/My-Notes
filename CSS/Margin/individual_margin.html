Using individual Margin properties

This div element has a top margin of 50px, a right margin of 20px, a bottom margin of 10px, and a left margin of 80px.

.cls1{ border: 1px solid black; margin-top: 50px; margin-right: 20px; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left: 80px; }

The margin shorthand property

Four values

This div element has a top margin of 25px, a right margin of 50px, a bottom margin of 75px, and a left margin of 100px.

.cls2{ border: 1px solid black; margin: 25px 50px 75px 100px; }

Three values

This div element has a top margin of 25px, a right and left margin of 50px, and a bottom margin of 75px.

.cls3{ border: 1px solid black; margin: 25px 50px 75px;}

Two values

This div element has a top and bottom margin of 25px, and a right and left margin of 50px.

.cls4{ border: 1px solid black; margin: 25px 50px; }

one value

This div element has a top, bottom, left, and right margin of 25px.

.cls5{ border: 1px solid black; margin: 25px; }
